Easy Homemade Doughnuts

Description

An easy recipe for doughnuts using ingredients that you likely already have at home.

This recipe only takes about 30 minutes to make and makes about 18 servings.

Ingredients

  • 3/8 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vineger
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons shortening
  • An egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 quart oil
  • 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir together milk and vineger; let stand untill thick.
  3. Cream together sugar and shortening untill smooth.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla until well-blended.
  5. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt into a seperate bowl; stir into sugar mixture alternating with milk mixture.
  6. Roll out dough onto floured surface to 1/3 inch thickness.
  7. Cut into doughnuts using a doughnut cutter. let stand for about 10 minutes.
  8. Heat oil in large, deep skillet to 375 degrees F. Fry donuts in hot oil in batches untill golden, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  9. Drain on paper towels.
  10. Dust with confectioner's sugar while warm and serve.
